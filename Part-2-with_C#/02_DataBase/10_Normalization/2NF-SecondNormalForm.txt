[2nd Normal Form]

=> is a further level of database normalization that buildss on the first normal form ruls.
--> It requires that each non-Key column in a table be functionally dependent in the entire primary key, not just a part  of it.

--> To Satisfy the requirments of 2NF:
[1]-> A Table must first be a 1NF, and Then Have:
[2]-> No Partial Dependencies: Each non-key Column in the Table must be Fully Dependent on the entire primary key.
